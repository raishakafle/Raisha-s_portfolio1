Above are some of my work, through out the semester. First off, I have the project where we had to work on creating our work based on responsive design, where we had to mention a fixed device width, so that whenever the tab gets minimised it can adjust according to the size of it. But, most importantly, varying on the device a particular user is using the size would be adjusted accordingly.

A responsive design is something that allows your website content to flow freely across all screen resolutions and sizes, and renders it to look great on all devices. It also makes it unnecessary to maintain different versions of your website for mobile and desktop and saves you time, resources and efforts.

For the second project, we were made familiar with how transitioning anything in html works. For which I added, a hover along with transitioning the image, which gave it a more sophisticated look which I really liked.

For the third project, we were encouraged to create a website describing ones hobbies, and skills. I wanted to challenge myself with a different color where I chose pink, mixing up with a touch of baby pink. At the end, the website actually turned out to be quite cute.

We were encoraged to be focusing on the text categories, including font weight, line height, font style and many more. Furthermore, we have been taught working with vertical, and horizontal navigations. We get to choose whichever works the best for us. That is what is giving in the fourth project.

The fourth project, was based on the sea life around the globe, where not only I learnt proper use of navigations but also some really fun facts about the sea life on earth.

Overall, this was a really fun project moving along with, and I am really proud of how everything has turned out.
